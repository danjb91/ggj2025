Welcome to Boba Holdings! We are Boba Holdings Ltd and in this game you play as 2 stock brokers duking it out in the video game stock exchange. Buy and sell stock by pulling and putting
boba stock into your cup. Win the game by having the most amount of cash before the time runs out!

Controls:
Player 1
WASD -> Move Straw
Q -> Use the straw to suck balls
Player 2
Arrow Keys -> Move Straw
/ -> Use the straw to suck balls

Open the project with Unity6
